Idli Recipe

Learn how to make delicious South Indian Idlis!

Calorie Value

Each Idli contains approximately 39 calories.

Steps to Make Idli

  1. Soak rice and urad dal (split black gram) overnight.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Let the batter ferment for 8-12 hours in a warm place.
  4. Grease the idli molds and pour the batter into them.
  5. Steam the idlis for 10-15 minutes until they are cooked.
  6. Serve hot with chutney or sambar!
