Boiled Eggs Recipe

Learn how to make perfectly boiled eggs!

Calorie Value

One boiled egg contains approximately 68 calories.

Steps to Make Boiled Eggs

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover them with cold water.
  2. Bring the water to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Once the water boils, reduce the heat and simmer for 9-12 minutes for hard-boiled eggs.
  4. After boiling, remove the eggs and place them in a bowl of ice water to cool.
  5. Peel the eggs and serve with salt, pepper, or any seasoning of choice!
